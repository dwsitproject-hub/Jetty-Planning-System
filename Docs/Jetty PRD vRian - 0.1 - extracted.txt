Lean Product Requirements Document: Jetty Planning System (JPS) Author: Rian Dharmawan | Date: 2026-03-03 1. Product Overview Vision / Goal: To digitize fragmented manual jetty operations into a unified system that eliminates financial leakage and provides real-time operational visibility. Target Users: Management , PPIC, Jetty Team, Tank Farm Team, and external partners (Agents/Surveyors). 2. Problem Statement The current reliance on Excel and WhatsApp for jetty management causes significant "blind spots" in the supply chain. Specifically, management lacks visibility into vessel nearness, resulting in extreme delays—such as the 8-day wait experienced by BG Mulia VII —and has no centralized way to audit SLAs or product loss/gain across shifts. 3. Success Metrics Wait-Time Reduction: Decrease the average gap between vessel arrival at anchorage and actual berthing. Demurrage Cost Savings: Lower monthly penalties paid to vessel owners by optimizing the jetty "Line-Up". Data Accuracy: Achieve 100% digital reconciliation of volume and quality data before a vessel is cleared to sail. 4. Scope In-Scope: AIS vessel tracking, digital berth planning, operational timesheets, automated quality/volume reconciliation, and management dashboards. Out-of-Scope: Automated physical valve control or refinery machinery automation. 5. Business or User Flow Ship Position Update: External Agents nominate vessels with ETA and load quality. Jetty Allocation: PPIC and Jetty Team plan the sequence based on refinery needs. Ship Docking: Recording of actual arrival and connection timestamps. Offloading: Detailed activity logging, including pumping and tank cleaning. Ship Discharge: Final reconciliation of volume/quality and issuance of "Dry Cert". 6. User Stories No Function User Story Acceptance Criteria 1 Dashboard As a member of Management , I want a one-pager dashboard that shows live jetty occupancy, 8-day wait alerts, and loss/gain percentages, so I can make rapid strategic decisions without reviewing multiple files. • Real-time map of all 5 berths. • Visual alert for vessels waiting >24 hours. • Aggregated Loss/Gain % for the last 10 shipments. 2 Nomination As an External Agent , I want to input vessel details (ETA, Qty, B/L Quality) into a portal, so the terminal can track my "nearness" automatically. • Mandatory fields for Vessel ID, ETA, and CPO Grade. • Automated timestamp for nomination receipt. 3 Planning As a PPIC Planner , I want to see real-time shore tank levels next to vessel ETAs, so I can prioritize feedstock for the refinery. • Integration of Tank Farm "Sounding" data. • Drag-and-drop prioritization on the "Line-Up" board. 4 Operations As a Jetty Team member, I want to log "Palka Cleaning" start and end times, so that the system can track operational bottlenecks. • Mobile-friendly input for each of the 15 palkas . • Automated calculation of total cleaning duration. 5 Quality As a QC Analyst , I want to upload CPO analysis (FFA, DOBI, IV) for each shipment, so that management can see quality degradation from load to discharge. • Comparison view of "Loading Quality" vs. "Discharge Quality". 6 Verification As an External Surveyor , I want to digitally sign the Dry Certificate , so the vessel is officially cleared to sail only after the tanks are clean. • System lock preventing "Vessel Sailed" status until Dry Cert is marked "CLEAN". 8. Dependencies / Risks Dependency: Accurate AIS data for real-time tracking. Risk: Low adoption from external agents/surveyors accustomed to paper. Mitigation: Provide a simplified mobile portal for external partners with automated PDF generation.
